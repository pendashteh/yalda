Together Through Distance

This Yalda Night holds special meaning for many of us. With loved ones overseas facing uncertainty and challenges, the longest night reminds us that even when separated by distance, we can still be there for each other.



We gather not to forget our worries, but to share them with people who understand. Whether your heart is heavy with news from family abroad, or you're simply feeling the weight of being far from those you love, this evening is for mutual support and quiet companionship.



Sometimes the most powerful thing we can do is simply sit together, acknowledging that we're not alone in our concerns for those we can't reach out and hold.
